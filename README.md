# SQL Business Case Study: Northwind Wholesale

## Business Context
I analysed order history for a wholesale food distributor to surface insights on revenue growth, customer value, product performance, and delivery efficiency.

---

## Key Findings
1. **USA generates 3x more revenue than any other market**, but Germany has the highest average order value, suggesting a premium opportunity.  
2. **Top 18 customers account for 40%+ of revenue**, creating significant concentration risk if any are lost to a competitor.  
3. **Shipping to certain countries averages 9+ days** vs a company norm of 3–4 days, suggesting a regional logistics problem.  
4. **23 customers have not ordered in 6+ months**, representing a churn risk addressable with a targeted retention campaign.
